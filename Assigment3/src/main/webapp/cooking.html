APPETIZER



Mini asparagus and mascarpone verrines Italian style


Ingredients:

10 frozen asparagus spears
6 candied tomatoes
1 stick of Bouillon Herbes de Provence
200g of mascarpone
100g of cottage cheese 40% (or more)
8 red radishes



Duration of the recipe : 15 min

Step 1
- Cook the asparagus 5 minutes in boiling water. Keep the tips and cut them into small pieces. Mix the mascarpone, the fromage blanc, the crumbled broth, the tomato coulis and the asparagus pieces.

Step 2
- Pour into 10 verrines. Decorate with radish slices and a touch of asparagus.

DESSERT

White chocolate, raspberry and pistachio millefeuille

Ingredients:

400g of raspberries
1 bar of 180g of white chocolate 8 sheets of Brick
500ml of semi-skimmed milk
2 eggs
1 tablespoon of cornstarch
100g of unsalted pistachios
2 tablespoons of brown sugar
1 lime
30g of butter

Duration of the recipe: 15 min

Step 1
In a saucepan, heat the milk and add the chocolate in it. Mix it. In a bowl, beat the eggs and the cornstarch. Pour the milk little by little and let it thicken on low heat for 5 minutes, stirring regularly. Place in the refrigerator for 1 hour. Add the zest of half a lime.

Step 2
Preheat your oven Th.7 (200°C). Cut 24 rectangles in the brick sheets. Brush them with butter and brown sugar. Overlay 2 sheets with crushed pistachios. Cook on the plate of your oven for 10 minutes.

Step 3
Alternate layers of brick pastry sheets, white chocolate cream and raspberries. Finish with raspberries.
