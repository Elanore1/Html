SPENDING A NIGHT WITH YOUR FAMILLY : WHAT TO DO ?

          


Friday or Saturday night is finally here! You'll be able to relax and enjoy a delicious evening with your family! Usually, you organize an aperitif, sometimes an improved dinner and sometimes you watch a series or a movie all together... But how about changing the program to spend a more original evening? There will be something for everyone and for every taste. Many activities are proposed to suit the youngest member of the family as well as the grandparents. Don't hesitate to let the kids help you plan your family night to make it more appealing. Once it becomes a habit, family night will probably become your favourite night of the week!







1 - The Post-it Game :


A classic of evenings with friends or family, the post-it game is simple.

You have to write on a post-it the name of a character, and stick it on the forehead of your right neighbor.The goal is to guess the name of the personality. The youngest player starts by asking a question. If the player gets a "yes" answer, he or she can continue with another question. If the answer is "no", it is the right-hand neighbor's turn to ask a question... until the table is full.
Between 3 and 8 players are needed for this game.





                              

2 - The beef bourgignon :

Beef bourguignon is one of the most emblematic recipes of French cuisine.
The old-fashioned recipe consists of a beef stew with carrots and mushrooms. And of course, a wine sauce!

It is a delicious and convivial dish that everyone can enjoy. However, it takes patience to eat it. Beef bourguignon is a simmered dish and the cooking of the beef requires a lot of time to melt.

3 - Virgin Spritz and Spritz :

It is from Italy, more particularly from the region of Venice that comes this cocktail very fashionable these last years.
The Spritz is composed of sparkling white wine (as long as it is possible, we take an Italian sparkling wine, the Prosecco), of Aperol or Campari (it is the alcohol which will bring the bitterness and the bright color of the drink) and of seltzer or equivalent.
For the little ones, the equivalent without alcohol is very appreciated.

Sparkling wines without alcohol are more and more widespread and are widely used in the preparation of mocktails (i.e. without alcohol). They have the enormous advantage of adding fine bubbles which are always very appreciated.
