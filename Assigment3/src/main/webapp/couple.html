SPENDING A NIGHT WITH YOUR OTHER HALF : WHAT TO DO?

          


Are you looking for a romantic and original activity to do in the evening at home? Discover our ideas to spend a special evening with your partner. Ideal to get out of the routine, for a Valentine's Day party, or simply to have fun together for a night! Find our tips for a couple's activity, while relaxing around a delicious meal.




1 - Taste discoveries :


To learn to trust your partner, this is a very good game! Although you can also do some dirty things to him... In any case, it's a guaranteed laugh!
The principle? Go shopping on your own and take 5 ingredients/foods each.
You will then have to blindfold your partner and make him taste the chosen food. Each wrong answer is equal to a dare.
The last time my boyfriend and I did this, he made me think he made me eat dog food when it was actually smoked herring! I thought I was going to kill him but we laughed a lot!








                       

2 - Chocolate cake :

It's a kid's favorite dessert, a foodie's favorite cake and the most comforting pastry there is.
The only constant? You'll have to melt the chocolate.
For the rest, the chocolate cake can be cooked in the microwave in a cup like a mug cake, or in the oven in a cake tin, either fondant or soft.
It can combine one, two or three colors of chocolate, be light or generous, be enriched with cream or hide chips...

  We told you, the chocolate cake is the pastry chef's must-have, the recipe to cook at the first
  moment of slackness or to spend a moment between lovers.
  Its only flaw? It doesn't last long!


3 - Champagne Soup :




The Champagne soup is a traditional aperitif of the Champagne region and a more luxurious version of the Angevin soup, which uses crémant as the main alcohol, not champagne.

This cocktail is preferred by consumers especially for New Year's Eve, but also during a romantic celebration.

It is a perfect preparation for festive moments and happy events!
